ABOUT US

Our Mission

The Center for Experimental Social Sciences (CESS) - India is a research laboratory affiliated with Nuffield College at Oxford University and housed at Auro University in Surat, Gujarat. It will open its doors in June 2016. CESS India aims to facilitate research in the social sciences by using rigorous, cross-disciplinary experimental techniques. A center where international collaboration is paramount, CESS India allows researchers from all over the world to use its state-of-the-art facilities to carry out experiments in the context of a major emerging nation.

A major feature of CESS India is its mobile lab initiative: CESS India maintains the technical equipment and local support necessary to carry out lab experiments in the field, particularly in relatively rural settings. In collaboration with its counterparts at Oxford University and at the University of Santiago, Chile, CESS India also provides the possibility of running the same experiment in three different parts of the world in order to ensure that results enjoy a high degree of external validity.

The first lab of its kind in the country, CESS India counts with affiliated faculty from major research universities, hosts PhD students and post-docs, and partners with other research institutions from all over the globe working in related fields. In addition, CESS India cultivates a spirit of continuous learning by organizing and participating in seminars, workshops, and an international conferences.
