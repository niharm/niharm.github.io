OUR MISSION

The Nuffield Centre for Experimental Social Sciences (CESS) - India is a research laboratory affiliated with Nuffield College at Oxford University and Auro University in Surat, Gujarat. It aims to facilitate research in the social sciences by using rigorous, cross-disciplinary experimental techniques in the context of a major emerging nation.



MOBILE LAB

We have the technical expertise and local support necessary to carry out lab-like experiments in the field, particularly in relatively rural settings.



USER STUDIES

With state-of-the-art facilities and counterparts at Oxford University and at the University of Santiago, Chile, we can run the same lab experiment in three different parts of the world simultaneously, ensuring results with a high degree of external validity.



GLOBAL NETWORK

We work with affiliated faculty from major research universities, host PhDs and Post-docs, and partner with research institutions from all over the globe.
