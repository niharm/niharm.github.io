ENGINEERS WITHOUT BORDERS | PRINCETON

With the group Engineers Without Borders, we aimed to build a water distribution system for a small community in Pitajaya, Peru.

I helped oversee the overall implementation of the first stage of the project, travelling over 3 trips to Peru in 2012 and 2013. We successfully built a water system for the first part of the community, Pitajaya Alta, by August 2013.
