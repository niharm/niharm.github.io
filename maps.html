A Map of Princeton: Missing Geographic Perspectives

Princeton publishes a list of undergraduates (the College Facebook), which any undergraduate can access. For each listing, you can see a Name, Year, and basic information. I scraped this info for 5224 students, which is essentially all of the 5,264 undergraduates at Princeton.

Exploring this, I found some interesting and suprising things about Princeton. I started by looking at the locations every person said they are from. For everyone, this gave a city, state, and country.

To start with, here's a map of every student at Princeton, with a dot for every person.

While it looks pretty cool, it's easy to see that this isn't an even distribution at all. There's of course a huge emphasis on the US, and there are parts of the world that don't have anyone here.
